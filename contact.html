Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our sarees and service.

How do I know if a saree is genuine Kanchipuram silk? +
Every Vastraluu saree comes with a Silk Mark certification issued by the Silk Mark Organisation of India (SMOI) — a government certification body. The silk mark tag is affixed to all our sarees before dispatch, guaranteeing 100% pure silk content. You can verify your certificate on the SMOI website using the unique code on your tag.
What is your return and exchange policy? +
We offer a hassle-free 15-day return and exchange policy. Items must be unused, in original condition, with all tags intact. To initiate a return, visit your account page or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days. Please note that customized or altered sarees cannot be returned.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we ship worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE typically take 7–12 business days via DHL Express. Import duties and taxes (if applicable) are borne by the customer and vary by country.
Can I get a saree custom-woven? +
Absolutely. We offer bespoke Kanchipuram silk sarees for special occasions such as weddings and milestone celebrations. Custom orders require a minimum lead time of 30–45 days and a 50% advance payment. Contact our design team via email at care@anayasilks.com to discuss your requirements and design preferences.
How should I care for my silk saree? +
Kanchipuram silk sarees should ideally be dry-cleaned by a specialist silk cleaner. If hand washing, use cool water with a gentle detergent designed for delicate fabrics. Never wring or tumble dry. Store your saree wrapped in white muslin cloth (never plastic) in a cool, dry place. Air your saree periodically to maintain its lustre.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders? +
Yes, we cater to boutiques, retailers, wedding planners, and corporate gifting. The minimum quantity for wholesale orders is 10 pieces. We offer attractive pricing for bulk orders along with custom labeling options. Please write to orders@anayasilks.com with your requirements for a custom quote.
